VisiORAL - Human Eye for the Visually Impaired

Project information

  • Linkedin Post: Click Here

A project aimed at transforming the visual world into the audio world for the blind and making them aware of the objects around them using Google's Cloud Vision API & a Raspberry Pi. This project has received over 250,000 views and 6000 likes across LinkedIn.

"The Changemaker" plaque was awarded by Dr. Amita Chauhan, Chairperson - Amity Group of Schools, in appreciation of the outstanding innovative approach to this project. Also bagged a gold medal in an inter-school competition in NCR & the first position with a cash prize in the District Science Exhibition organised by the state government. This tries to transform the visual world into the audio world for the blind.
